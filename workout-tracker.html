üéØ Workout Tips for Best Results

  • Warm up: Always spend 5-10 minutes warming up (light cardio, dynamic stretches)
  • Form over speed: Focus on proper form rather than rushing through exercises
  • Progressive overload: Gradually increase difficulty by adding reps or time
  • Rest days are crucial: Your muscles need recovery time to grow stronger
  • Stay hydrated: Drink water before, during, and after workouts
  • Consistency is key: Missing one day won't ruin progress, but make it the exception

ü•ó Nutrition Guidelines

  • Protein intake: Aim for 1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight daily
  • Reduce refined carbs: Focus on whole grains, vegetables, and fruits
  • Healthy fats: Include avocados, nuts, olive oil, and fatty fish
  • Caloric deficit for fat loss: Aim for 300-500 calorie deficit from maintenance
  • Meal timing: Eat protein within 2 hours after workout for recovery
  • Limit processed foods: Focus on whole, nutrient-dense foods
